# Research on Rapid English Sentence Learning Method Based on Large Language Models and AIGC for Chinese Students

**EnglishUP — AI-Powered English Sentence Learning**

[English](#english) | [中文](#chinese)

---

## English

**EnglishUP** is an AI-powered English learning system that helps Chinese students truly master English through sentence-level deep learning — not just memorizing words.

### The Problem

Over 95% of English classrooms in China rely on traditional methods: vocabulary drills and grammar lectures. Students can recognize thousands of words but struggle to write a single correct sentence. A 40-student class typically gives individual feedback to fewer than 5 students per lesson, with delays of several days.

### Our Solution

We combine three AI technologies into a closed-loop learning system:

| Technology | Role |
|---|---|
| **LLM** (GPT, DeepSeek) | Real-time 3D evaluation: grammar, logic, nativeness |
| **AIGC** (Video Generation) | 15-second cinematic short videos as episodic memory anchors |
| **Ebbinghaus Algorithm** | Spaced repetition of complete sentences, not just words |

### Five-Step Sentence Learning Method

```
Word Meaning → Collocations → Sentence Structure → Grammar → AI-Assisted Imitation Writing
```

Each step builds on the previous one. The true test of mastery: can the student independently write a correct sentence? With AI providing instant feedback at every step, students who couldn't complete this loop before now can.

### Experimental Validation (n=141)

A 12-week controlled experiment in Wuzhong, Ningxia (rural China):

| Group | Method | Grammar | Retention (2wk) |
|-------|--------|:-------:|:---------------:|
| A | Traditional classroom | 3.18/5 | 44.1% |
| B | Traditional learning device | 3.62/5 | 64.3% |
| C | **EnglishUP (experimental)** | **4.28/5** | **80.5%** |

All differences significant at p<0.001. Effect size (Cohen's d) = 1.28 for nativeness.

### Key Insight

> "Words are truly mastered only when you can form correct sentences with them. Sentences are truly mastered only when you can create your own. Before AI, most students couldn't complete this loop. Now they can."

## 中文

**EnglishUp** 是一个 AI 驱动的英语学习系统，旨在帮助中国学生通过句子层面的深度学习真正掌握英语——而非死记硬背单词。

### 问题

中国 95% 以上的英语课堂采用传统模式：词汇背诵 + 语法讲解。学生能认出几千个单词，却写不出一句正确的英文。一个 40 人的班级，每节课通常只有不到 5 人能获得教师书面批改反馈，延迟可达数天。

### 解决方案

我们将三种 AI 技术整合为一个闭环学习系统：

| 技术 | 作用 |
|---|---|
| **LLM** 大语言模型 | 秒级三维评价：语法正确性、逻辑合理性、表达地道性 |
| **AIGC** 视频生成 | 为每个单词和例句生成 15 秒电影级短视频，建立情境记忆锚点 |
| **艾宾浩斯遗忘曲线** | 将完整句子纳入间隔重复复习，而非仅记忆单词 |

### 五步句子学习法

```
理解单词 → 理解短语 → 理解句型结构 → 搞清语法 → AI 辅助仿写
```

层层递进。单词真正掌握的标志是能造出正确的句子，句子真正掌握的标志是能仿写出自己的句子。之前学生难以独立完成这一闭环，现在 AI 在每一步提供即时辅助与反馈。

### 实验验证（n=141）

在宁夏吴忠地区初中开展为期 12 周的三组对照实验：

| 组别 | 方法 | 语法得分 | 两周后保持率 |
|------|------|:----:|:----------:|
| A 组 | 传统课堂 | 3.18/5 | 44.1% |
| B 组 | 传统学习机 | 3.62/5 | 64.3% |
| C 组 | **EnglishUp（实验组）** | **4.28/5** | **80.5%** |

所有差异均达 p<0.001 水平，表达地道性效应量 d=1.28。

### 核心观点

> 学透单词看造句，学透句子看仿写。AI 让每个学生都能走完这条路。
